> In the summer <br/>
> I stretch out on the shore <br/>
> And think of you. <br/>
> Had I told the sea <br/>
> What I felt for you,<br/>
> It would have left its shores,<br/>
> Its shells,<br/>
> Its fish,<br/>
> And followed me.<br/>


&mdash; Qabbani
